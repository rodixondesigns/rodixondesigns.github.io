About

Here at H & H General Contracting, we believe that by focusing on each project’s long-term stability, functionality, and efficiency we are in turn ensuring the clients satisfaction and return on investment; thus becoming their preferred contractor. Over time this results in the best type of business, one that drives itself through repeat customers and word of mouth referrals.

We guarantees every project is built to meet or exceed code specifications with long-term sustainability and value.

Todd Hollins

A life-long resident of Southern Indiana, Todd Hollins began working with his Uncle Pete installing and servicing heating and air systems at an early age. During high school Todd jumped at the opportunity to spend half his day outside, building a 1500 sq ft home, in-lieu-of class. In doing so, he received a Building Trades certificate with his diploma.

With his Uncle Pete’s encouragement, Todd went on to obtain an Associate’s Degree in Land Survey from Vincennes University. He also earned a bachelors of science from Indiana University Southeast. After Graduating in 2007, Todd worked closely with commercial General Contractors and Construction Managers as a Construction Surveyor and Field Engineer until deciding to start his own company. Throughout it all, Todd has always been the family and friend’s go-to man for residential improvements and repairs.

As a proud father, Todd hopes H & H General Contracting will serve as an example to his daughter that hard work and a commitment to one’s principles are the keys to success, no matter the endeavor.

Pete Howard

Today, Uncle Pete serves as H & H General Contracting’s Chief Consultant, Estimator, and Head of Quality Control. Together Todd and Pete agree their business will build solely on the foundation of honesty, integrity, and reliability.
