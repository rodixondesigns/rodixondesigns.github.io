Our Promise


We’re a local grassroots business based in Georgetown Indiana. H & H General Contracting Incorporates traditional craftsmanship, attention to detail, and professional integrity with modern methods of building science, specifically the idea of the "House as a System”, to provide a unique service focused on Total Home Performance, not the sale of a particular improvement or trade. HHGC offers Hourly Residential Maintenance and Repairs Service, as well as General Contracting services, to provide long-term solutions and value to clients interested in Improving and Maintaining their Home's Systems Performance.

Todd Hollins owns and operates H&H General Contracting. He's a family man from Southern Indiana that runs his business with honesty and integrity. Welcoming someone into your home and entrusting them with it can be scary. You can rest assured that Todd and his crew have your best iterests in mind at all times, and will respect you and your property first and foremost.

Todd Hollins
