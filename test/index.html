Origins

For centuries, explorers who travel the waterways have known of the river eddy. A place where the water goes against the flow and creates a calm. After the rapids, whitewater kayakers immediately seek the "eddy" to rest, regroup, and plan for their next line down the river. Kölkin is the Danish word for "river eddy." Kölkin exists to give you the same place for rest, regrouping, and planning in a welcoming community with the best coffee we can provide.
