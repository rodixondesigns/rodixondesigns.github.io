Resumé

Hi, I'm Ro

Lead Designer

Hey There!

I have 10+ years of experience solving tough problems for end users, through user-centric design and research.

The first nine years of my career I was an in-house designer in the healthIT space, and I've spent the last five years consulting on an array of products from Crypto/Fintech, to edtech and so many industries and clients in between.

I love where I'm at in my career, because I have enough experience to be considered an expert in my field, and yet I still feel I have so much to learn!

Qualifications

Lead

  • I lead a multi-disciplinary team on client projects while staying on budget and on schedule.

  • I mentor junior designers, empowering them to do their best work. I guide them to set and reach goals that benefit them personally and align with team and company objectives.

Design

  • I have a great deal of experience with UCD, goal-centered design, various methodologies, and user testing practices.

  • My career started off designing SaaS and B2B solutions in healthcare, and I've spent the last 5 years as a consultant helping clients design solutions that meet business goals while exceeding needs of their users.

  • I've had the opportunity to work in a broad array of industries, from EdTech, FinTech, and Political-Charged social media design to digital solutions in manufacturing and construction just to name a few.

Research

  • I've gained a great deal of experience over the years with usability testing methods, and am well versed in the differing standardized options available today.

  • I find or collect behavioral metrics whenever possible to make data-driven design decisions.
