Why hire me?

I like working with individuals and smaller to medium-sized businesses, because I'm uniquely qualified to offer them a web design experience that they normally would not be able to afford. Larger design firms starting price is typically $10,000 or more. I can't do everything that larger companies can, but for what most smaller businesses need, I'm perfect! I can design, develop, and test a beautiful website, and you won't have to pay me $10,000 to do it.

If you'd like to meet in person to discuss your next project, I'm located in the Kentuckiana area. Too far? I'm willing to work remote if you are!
