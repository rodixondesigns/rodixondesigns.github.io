Who am I?

Hi, I'm Ro, User Experience (UX) Designer by day and Web developer by night. I've spent the last eight years in UX working on SAAS applications, and two years in Front-End Development.

I'd love to help you with your website!

Why me?

I'm uniquely qualified to offer small business owners a web design experience that they normally could not afford. I can't do everything that larger companies can, but I can design and develop a beautiful website, and you won't have to pay me $10,000 to do it.

With me you get a personal touch that angencies just can't offer.

Services

Web Design

I create crisp, responsive, and fast HTML websites from scratch and can host them for free! I pride myself on the ability to cater the design to suite you/your business's personality. You won't get stale or "out of the box" websites from me!

SEO

If you're going to have a website these days, it has to be optimized for search engines, like Google, to find when your target audience completes a search. I'll work with you to make sure we're targeting the audience you want!

Social Media

I can work with you to ensure that your social media accounts are synced with one another, as well as your website so you're not duplicating your efforts and your marketing message remains consistent.

Process

Consultation

We'll get together over coffee or video chat to discuss what you envision, what you hope to accomplish, and I can also answer any questions you may have.

Design

I'll design your website based on our discussion during the initial consultation. Depending on which package you selected you're entitled to a certain number of revisions as well.

Develop

Once you're completely happy with the design, I'll start developing your website. I'll publish it to a test domain so I can ensure it renders fast and beautifully on all devices, and send you a link for review.

Publish

This is the exciting part- publishing your site for all to love and see! Get ready for some 'ooohs' and 'aaahs'!
