Qualifications

  • I am well versed in the differing standardized usability testing methods.
  • I often use behavioral metrics in making intelligent design decisions.
  • As outlined in my resume, I also have experience in Product Management, QA, Instructional Design, Front End Development, and Email Marketing.
  • I have a deep understanding of humankind and am a great active listener. My ability to empathize with folks from all walks of life has made me a better designer.
  • I have excellent written and verbal communication skills, and a knack for knowing how to best reach a particular audience.

Experience

2016 - Present
UX Designer at Waystar

I am the primary UX designer for Waystar’s Patient Tools line of products. The majority of my time is spread across discovery, research, and design. I’m responsible for our user feedback forum which is a monthly email where we provide users helpful content and offer remote usability testing. I was also responsible for implementing an SUS (system usability scale) method of standardized usability testing across our product line. I have a lot of experience using behavioral metrics and google analytics to make intelligent design decisions.

2012 - 2016
Programmer Analyst, Kindred

At the time, Kindred didn’t have product managers or designers- they had analysts. My job was to meet with users and gather requirements, design the app, write the BRs and test plan, monitor devs, complete DEV testing, and support the app throughout go- live. We were responsible for the entire PDLC of our products from the discovery, to the design and development.

2009 - 2012
Intern, Kindred

I interned in Kindred’s HR/Payroll IT Department where we designed all HR and Payroll systems. I wore a lot of hats, including:
- Instructional Designer: I wrote and designed all training materials.
- Project Management: I was selected by my peers to lead the Intern Video project, where we shot, edited, and published a marketing video for Kindred’s Intern program.

2007 - 2009
Associate, GFS Marketplace

I excelled in all roles and in 2008 was awarded the Associate of the Year award.
