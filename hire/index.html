Why me?

My experience and skillset would make me an excellent candidate for a UX/Product Design position- but that could be said about a lot of people. Here are a few reasons you should choose me over the competition.

  • I'm one of the most hardworking people you'll ever meet. I've had less than 7 days off in a row since my first job started 11 years ago, and it's because I like going to work everyday.
  • I have an immeasurable amount of empathy, which not only makes me fun to work with but it helps me build a rapport with users to gather better feedback, and design better solutions.
  • Finally, I'm a genuinely good person with a big heart. I hope hiring good and ethical people is important to you and your company.
